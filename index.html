Rules

Objective: Guide Pac-Man through the maze, eating all pellets while avoiding ghosts.

Gameplay Elements: Pac-Man has only one life. Losing a life ends the game. Difficulty levels increase as Pac-Man consumes pellets. Ghosts' speed increases with each difficulty level.

Movement: Control Pac-Man with arrow keys (←↑→↓). Pac-Man moves through the maze horizontally and vertically.

Pellets: Eat all white pellets to progress through the game.

Power Pellets: Larger, flashing pellets make ghosts vulnerable. Pac-Man can eat ghosts for extra points while they're vulnerable.

Ghosts: Avoid being caught by ghosts (Blinky, Pinky, Inky, Clyde). Ghosts capture Pac-Man upon contact

Maze Warp Tunnels: Use warp tunnels for quick traversal across the maze.

Scoring: Points awarded for eating pellets and ghosts. No fruit feature.

Level Completion: Progress through difficulty levels instead of traditional levels. Game ends when Pac-Man loses a life.
